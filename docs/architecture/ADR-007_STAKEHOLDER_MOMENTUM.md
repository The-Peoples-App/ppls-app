# ADR-007: High-Visibility Stakeholder Engagement Framework

## Status
Proposed

## Context
HALA is transitioning from a 20-year legacy of print-based advocacy to a modern digital ecosystem. To maintain alignment between high-velocity engineering cycles and Board-level strategic goals, we require a communication bridge that translates technical milestones into tangible organizational value.

## Decision
We will implement a "Momentum-First" reporting cadence:
1. **The "Live-Link" Demo:** Utilize Expo EAS "Internal Distribution" to provide Board members with persistent, installable Alpha builds on their physical devices.
2. **Value-Based Reporting:** Transition from administrative task-tracking to "Product Milestone" reporting (e.g., "Feature: CalFresh Logic Engine Complete").
3. **The "Strategic Slide":** A high-fidelity visual asset produced by the UX Lead for every sprint, designed for immediate inclusion in HALA’s annual reports and grant proposals.

## Consequences
- **Pro:** Solidifies stakeholder trust by providing "touchable" digital progress.
- **Pro:** Empowers HALA leadership with professional assets for donor engagement.
- **Con:** Requires ~2 hours per sprint for technical-to-strategic translation.
